# Launch Kit — Enterprise AI Evaluation Operating Model Template

## Asset title
Enterprise AI Evaluation Operating Model Template

## Positioning
A practical template for enterprise teams that need to evaluate AI systems like operating systems, not lab demos. It gives architects, delivery leads, and platform teams a structured way to define what to measure, how to review changes, and what evidence is required before an AI system should be trusted in production.

## Target audience
- enterprise AI architects
- delivery and transformation leads
- AI platform teams
- governance / risk / control owners
- RAG and agent solution teams moving from pilot to production

## What’s included
- evaluation layers spanning business outcomes, quality, retrieval, tool usage, policy, operations, and release governance
- scenario inventory structure
- golden-set / review-set design guidance
- weighted scoring model template
- release gate design table
- telemetry and evidence capture checklist
- failure taxonomy and threshold rules
- operating cadence for weekly, monthly, and quarterly reviews

## Why this asset matters
A lot of enterprise AI teams say they have an evaluation strategy when what they really have is a handful of test prompts and some anecdotal reviewer feedback.

That is not an evaluation operating model.

Production AI systems need teams to answer questions like:
- what counts as a must-pass scenario?
- what evidence is required before a prompt or model change ships?
- how do we measure groundedness, policy compliance, and tool validity together?
- how do we connect answer quality to business outcomes?
- what failure categories trigger rollback, review, or redesign?

This asset makes those decisions concrete.
